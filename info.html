information

Welcome to RISK Esports. A small creativity based organization, which started as a small editing team, back in 2023. We're seeking to make a difference in the Brawlhalla community. We have a heavy focus on content production and artistic value. Check out our editors, content creators, and competitive players.

alliance

RISK empowers the Brawl community by offering benefits to those who join the RISK alliance council. Tier 1 allies recieve monetary sponsorships, free designs (thumbnails, banners, logos, etc), tournament organization help, coaching, and full support from RISK. Tier 2 allies recieve almost the same benefits, except without any monetary benefits. Tier 3 allies only recieve design related benefits and/or the ability to also utilize RISK organization members for future events.
